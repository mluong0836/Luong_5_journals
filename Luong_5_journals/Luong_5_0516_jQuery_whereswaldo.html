I have some picture motifs that I have taken into consideration before deciding on my actual
image. I first thought of dogs because there are many kinds of dogs out there that can blend in
with the setting, since they have a variety of fur color and texture, suitable for camoflaugining.
Also, dogs are my favorite household pets as well. In addition, another motif that I was thinking was
the ocean and marine life. I was thinking of using an image of an ocean with many blue whales stacked on
top of the background. Therefore, the whales blend in the blue ocean setting and there are a variety of blue
whales in the world; thus, providing more challenges for the player to find the difference among these extremely
similar whales. In the end, I chose a whale because of the above reasons and that it reminds me of going to the beach,
which I have been longing to go, since winter. Therefore, there's a more meaningful purpose for this marine theme.
