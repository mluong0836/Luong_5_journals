Our customers have recently requested that we provide some background informatio about our company. This convinced us that we should provide a page to discuss about the creators of this website and Apple Inc. Prepare to be amazed at the information given in the next few tabs under About Apple. You will learn much more about us as you navigate through our pages, but here is a brief description of the authors of this wonderful product line and website.

Our creator, Steve Jobs, was born in San Francisco, California, where Steve began pondering his purpose in life. This thought was finally presented to reality when the first Apple computer was created and introduced to the public. Apple started out as a small company in a garage and grew to become one of the largest corporations in the world.

This website was sponsered by Micheal Luong and redesigned to incorporate more colors than the original white apple website. Many customers have probably wondered why we used white not any other colors. The answer is time. Apple is a very busy company and invests most of its time on product lines. Luckily, we found a student with a lot of downtime, so he was asked to create a multi-colored, picturesque website, which we all can view now.
