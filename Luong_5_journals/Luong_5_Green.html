Green evokes a sense of peace and tranquility and is easy on the human eye. It is represents wealth, since money is represented by the color green. Furthermore, because green is the universal color of nature, many pictures include tints of greens to indicate the outdoor setting. Also, green can be easily conceived by the human eye and brain, which is why green means “go” like in green traffic lights. On the other hand, the color green can also have negative connotations like representing greed and envy.
